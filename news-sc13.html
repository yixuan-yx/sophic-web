PSDC Industry 4.0 Week 2018

We are pleased to be one of the participants in PSDC Industry 4.0 Week organized by PSDC and EEPN on 13th and 14th November 2018. It is an annual event to create awareness on the new wave of Industrial Revolution and provide a platform for networking, forming future collaborations and driving market adoption among manufacturers, vendors, system integrators and consumers. We enjoyed meeting everyone there and were glad that people had the chance to see our products during the exhibition.

For anyone who dropped by our booth, we would like to say thank you and look forward to the opportunity of serving you!
