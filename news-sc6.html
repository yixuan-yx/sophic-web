In Collaboration With Politech

Expose new generation to industry 4.0
Sophic not only aims to be global specialist but help young generation to develop & enhance their creative & innovative potential in rapidly growing technology. Sophic which is known for its expertise in Internet of Things had collaborated with Politeknik Balik Pulau to provide students and lecturers the opportunity of exposing the elements of IoT.
As Internet of Things (IoT) is rapidly penetrating all aspects of our lives and underpinning more and more industries, it will provide various career opportunities to young generation especially fresh graduates.
