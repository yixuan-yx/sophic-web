Smart Manufacturing & Automation Technology 2017

Sophic Automation is pleased to announce that our director Mr Koh Dim Kuan has been invited as a panel speaker for Seminar on Smart Manufacturing and Automation Technology at MIDA’s Perdana Hall.
This event was held in conjunction with MIDA’s 50 years of anniversary celebration to promote investments in the new and challenging era of global connectivity and revolutionary approach of doing business. It focused on the ecosystem required to adopt automation and smart manufacturing technology in Malaysia.
The Industrial Internet of Things and Smart Manufacturing era has arrived and it will continue to evolve and converging new and disruptive technologies. This new direction is highly-connected and knowledge-enabled, where devices and processes are connected, monitored, and optimized.
