FMM ICT Conference 2017

Moving Towards Industry 4.0: Exploring the World of Connected Enterprise
Sophic Automation had been selected as one of the local SME to participated in FMM ICT Conference 2017, an event that supported by MDEC, which aimed to create the awareness among Malaysian manufacturers on the latest manufacturing and ICT Technology.
We had been given an opportunity to showcase our high-tech products and services which can improve one`s business efficiency and efficiently. Through this session, we shared our view and advice on utilizing high-tech in improving their business.
