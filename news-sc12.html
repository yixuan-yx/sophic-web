IoT Forum 2018

On 11 December 2018, we participated in IoT Malaysia Forum 2018 organized by Intel Malaysia, in collaboration with MITI and MIDA. The objective of this forum was to review the progress of IoT, highlight Smart City innovation with IoT, and discuss how IoT can be the catalyst for Industry 4.0 deployment. Apart from this, we had the great chance to meet Datuk Darell Leiking and we were proud to showcase our product and solution.

We would like to thank everyone who dropped by our booth. It was a perfect opportunity to meet each and every one of you!
